# AI-Assisted Job Search

---

## AI Usage Overview
Artificial intelligence tools were used to analyze cybersecurity job descriptions, identify required technical skills, and optimize resume wording for applicant tracking systems (ATS).

---

## Practical Applications
- Resume keyword optimization
- Job description analysis
- Interview preparation guidance

AI was used as a productivity and analysis tool only. All technical knowledge, portfolio content, and project work reflect personal learning and hands-on effort.
